Recipe 1: Chocolate Chip Cookies
Name: Chocolate Chip Cookies
Cuisine Type: American
Taste: Sweet
Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Ingredients:

2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup unsalted butter, melted
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 large eggs
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
Mix flour and baking soda in a small bowl.
In a large bowl, combine butter, sugars, vanilla, and salt. Add eggs and mix well.
Gradually add the flour mixture and stir until combined.
Fold in chocolate chips.
Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown.
Recipe 2: Spaghetti Carbonara
Name: Spaghetti Carbonara
Cuisine Type: Italian
Taste: Savory
Preparation Time: 25 minutes
Ingredients:

400g spaghetti
2 large eggs
100g Parmesan cheese, grated
100g pancetta, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil
Instructions:

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese.
Heat olive oil in a pan and cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and sauté briefly.
Toss cooked spaghetti in the pan with pancetta.
Remove from heat, quickly mix in the egg mixture, and add reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
Recipe 3: Veggie Stir-Fry
Name: Veggie Stir-Fry
Cuisine Type: Asian
Taste: Umami
Preparation Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients:

2 cups broccoli florets
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 cup snap peas
1 carrot, julienned
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
1 teaspoon ginger, minced
1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
Instructions:

Heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet.
Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute.
Add broccoli, bell pepper, snap peas, and carrot. Stir-fry for 5 minutes.
Mix in soy sauce and the cornstarch slurry. Cook until sauce thickens.
Serve hot over rice or noodles.
Recipe 4: Banana Pancakes
Name: Banana Pancakes
Cuisine Type: American
Taste: Sweet
Preparation Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1 ripe banana, mashed
1 large egg
2 tablespoons melted butter
Instructions:

In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
In another bowl, whisk milk, mashed banana, egg, and melted butter.
Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
Heat a non-stick pan and pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
Serve with syrup or fresh fruit.
Recipe 5: Caprese Salad
Name: Caprese Salad
Cuisine Type: Italian
Taste: Fresh
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Ingredients:

2 large tomatoes, sliced
200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
Fresh basil leaves
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a plate.
Add fresh basil leaves between the slices.
Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
